My passion is design! I started looking at many outlets to curb my creative thirsts, and with design, I found it. For the past several years I’ve been honing my skills designing for both print and web. I’m always striving to learn more to improve my craft.

I believe design and art come from how a person feels. I draw inspiration for my work based on the feelings I get when talking with a client. Using this skill and pairing it with current design trends has greatly aided me.

An integral part of my process is to get my ideas down on paper. As a right-brained thinker, I need to see my work on paper before I can bring it to life digitally. I often ask myself the question is “how can I make that better?” I believe that with the many facets that I’m interested in gives me an edge in the left-brained world that I’m trying to enter.
